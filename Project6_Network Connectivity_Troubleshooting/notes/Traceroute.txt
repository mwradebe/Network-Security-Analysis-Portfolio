Hop #,IP Address,Who is this?,What's happening?

1,172.19.0.1,WSL Gateway,"This is the virtual ""exit"" from your Linux environment into Windows. Notice it responded here! (Unlike your previous ping)."

2,192.168.0.1,Your Home Router,This is the physical box in your house/office. Your data has officially left your computer and hit the hardware.

3 & 4,* * *,Hidden Routers,"These are routers (likely at your ISP) that are configured to be ""invisible."" They pass your data but refuse to identify themselves for security."

5,10.195.18.1,ISP Network,You are now inside your Internet Service Provider's (ISP) private infrastructure.

8,192.178.74.198,Google's Front Door,"You've reached Google's massive global network (the ""Edge"")."

9,142.251.47.46,Google.com,Destination reached! This is the specific server that handled your request.


Low numbers (under 5ms): You are still on your own computer.

Higher numbers (20ms+): data is physically traveling long distances over fiber-optic cables.
